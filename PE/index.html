With this in mind here is a list of 4 sports players from India hailing from different states and their 2 achievements.

First on the list is Kapil Dev.
Hailing from Punjab, he is India's first fast bowler.
Under his captaincy India won the 1983 cricket World Cup.

Second on the list Major Dhyan Chand who hails from Uttar Pradesh is regarded as one of the best hockey players in history.
He has won 3 Olympic gold medals and was also awarded the Padma Bhushan in 1956.

Third is Sunil Chhetr who comes from Telangana has the record of scoring the highest goals scored by an Indian.
He is also the third-highest international goalscorer among active football players.

Last but not the least, Mary Kom who hails from the state of Manipur is the only woman to win the World Amateur Boxing Championship six times.
She became the first Indian female boxer to win a gold medal in the Asian Games.
